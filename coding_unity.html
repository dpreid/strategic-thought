Diffusion Limited Aggregation simulation

I wanted to test out Unity as a possibel resource in creating educational simulations for teaching physics. As a first test of its potential, I simulated Diffusion Limited Aggregation in 3D. In later iterations I may add calculations of the fractal dimension of the growth formed, but for now the interactivity is limited to changing the attractive potential of the central "seed".
